Establishment Map

See all food establishments near CvSU-Bacoor — tap a pin to view details or get directions.

Choose your avatar icon
Before showing your location, pick your gender icon — tap 🧔 Male or 👩 Female using the toggle buttons at the bottom of the map. This determines what icon appears on your exact location.
2
Show your location
Click 📍 Show My Location at the bottom of the map. Allow location permission when prompted — your chosen avatar will appear at your exact position on the map in real-time.
3
Switch icon anytime
Even after your location is shown, you can tap the 🧔 / 👩 buttons to instantly swap your avatar icon — no need to re-tap Show My Location. The change happens in real-time.
4
Tap an establishment pin
🟢 Green pins = Open (can order now)  ·  🔴 Red pins = Closed. Tap any pin to view details, menu, and get walking directions.
5
Get walking directions
After tapping a pin, click Get Directions. A turn-by-turn walking route will appear with distance and estimated walk time. Click Clear Route to remove it.
6
Filter & change map type
Use Filter by Category on the left sidebar to narrow down establishments. Use the 🗺️ Layers button (top-right of map) to switch between Hybrid, Street, Satellite, or Terrain view.
